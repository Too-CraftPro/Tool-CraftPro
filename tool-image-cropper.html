How to Crop Your Image

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag your file to the upload box or click to select it.
  2. Adjust Crop Area: A draggable crop box will appear. Drag the corners or edges to resize it, or move the entire box.
  3. Use Controls: Use the buttons for fixed aspect ratios or enter exact pixel coordinates in the manual input boxes for precision.
  4. Crop and Download: When ready, click "Crop Image". Your new file will download automatically.
