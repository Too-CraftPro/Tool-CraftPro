How to Use the Image Converter

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag and drop your image file or click the upload box to select it.
  2. Choose Options: Select your desired output format (like PNG or JPG). If available, adjust the quality slider.
  3. Click "Convert Now": The tool will process the image instantly in your browser.
  4. Download Your File: After conversion, a "Download File" button will appear. Click it to save your new image.
  5. Start Over: Click "New Conversion" to reset the tool and upload another image.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No, absolutely not. Our Image Converter is a browser-based tool. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring 100% privacy and security.

What's the difference between JPG, PNG, and WEBP?

JPG is best for photographs. PNG is great for graphics and supports transparency. WEBP is a modern format that offers excellent quality at a smaller file size, perfect for the web.
