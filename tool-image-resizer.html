How to Resize Your Images

  1. Upload an Image: Drag your file to the upload box or click to select it.
  2. Choose a Method: Select "By Pixels" or "By Percentage".
  3. Enter Dimensions:
    • For Pixels: Enter your desired width or height. The other dimension will update automatically if the aspect ratio lock is on. Click the lock icon to unlock and set them independently.
    • For Percentage: Use the slider to scale your image up or down.
  4. Resize and Download: Click "Resize Image". A download button will appear. Click it to save your new file.

About Our Free Image Resizer

Whether you're preparing images for a website, a social media post, or an email newsletter, having the right dimensions is crucial. Our Free Image Resizer makes it incredibly simple to change the size of any image without needing complicated software. You can scale your images to exact pixel dimensions or by a simple percentage.

A key feature of our tool is the "maintain aspect ratio" lock. By default, it's enabled to ensure that when you change an image's width, the height adjusts proportionally (and vice versa). This prevents your images from looking stretched or squashed. For advanced use cases, you can unlock it to set custom dimensions. All processing happens securely in your browser, guaranteeing your privacy.
